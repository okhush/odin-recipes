Lasagna Recipe.

Description

the best lasagna ever, i guess.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef
  • 2 cups (16 oz) marinara sauce
  • 2 cups (16 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups (16 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • salt and pepper pepper pepper

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, and garlic; add back the beef
  4. Make the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Assemble the lasagna
  7. Bake
